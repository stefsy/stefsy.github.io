Core Workflows

Four practical ways to integrate AI into your daily tasks.

1. Voice, Memos, and Mobility

Your voice is faster than your fingers. Use it for more than just dictation to capture and structure thoughts on the move.

Demo 1: Fast Typing: Use "Superwhisper" at the desk to draft content quickly.

Demo 2: Instant Memos: Convert a raw voice memo transcript into a structured, readable summary with a single prompt.

2. Supercharged Research

Move beyond simple searching to have AI synthesize information and map knowledge from trusted sources.

Demo 3: Trusted Research: Use Perplexity Pro's "Focus" feature to query specific sources like academic papers or official docs.

Demo 4: Knowledge Mapping: Upload documents to NotebookLM to generate instant summaries, FAQs, and even audio "podcasts".

3. Your Second Brain in ChatGPT

Evolve ChatGPT from a generic tool into a personalized assistant that knows your work, style, and company context.

Demo 5: Context-Aware Emails: Turn a messy meeting transcript into a polished client email that reflects your company's tone and project details.

Demo 6: Hiring Support: Feed a job description, company values, and a resume to ChatGPT to generate highly specific screening questions.

4. Custom AI Coaches

Build a specialized coach that uses expert methods and your personal learning history to provide targeted feedback.

Demo 7: Personalized Coding Coach: Prime an AI with specific teaching methods (e.g., Polya's method) and your own log of past coding challenges to get tailored guidance on new problems.

Key Concepts & Gotchas

Guiding principles for getting the most out of these tools.

Three Things to Remember

  • ✓

    ChatGPT is not Google.

    It's a reasoning engine. Use it to synthesize, create, and transform information, not just find it.

  • ✓

    Make prompts reusable.

    Build a personal library of your best prompts. This saves time and ensures consistent, high-quality results.

  • ✓

    Collect context on yourself.

    The more personal data and examples you provide, the more personalized and useful the AI's output becomes.

The Big "Gotcha": These Are Self-CHECK Tools

You are the final quality control. The AI is only as smart as the context and review you provide. Always check for hallucinations, laziness, or nonsensical answers. Verify critical information before using it.
